# Privacy Policy for EzApply: ADHD Apply Tracker

**Last Updated:** January 2, 2026

EzApply ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension handles your data.

**1. Data Collection and Storage**
EzApply operates as a local-only extension.
* **No Remote Servers:** We do not operate any backend servers.
* **Local Storage:** All job application data (titles, companies, URLs, notes) is stored entirely on your local device using the Chrome Storage API (`chrome.storage.local`).
* **No Data Sharing:** We do not collect, sell, or share your data with third parties.

**2. Permissions**
The extension requires the following permissions to function:
* `storage`: To save your job list locally.
* `activeTab` / `scripting`: To automatically capture job details from LinkedIn/Indeed when you are active on those pages.
